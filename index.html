Welcome

Chat314 is your personal home page with a built in AI assitant. Chat314 uses a simple neural network to meet your needs and answer your questions. The neural network sends questions to different models to complete your tasks efficiently.

How it works

Source code
Chat314 combines multiple API's and AI models to create an all-in-one customizable and portable AI assistant. Chat314 uses the Classy-AI python package to act as a router for these models. The model tokenizes your input and decides on the best option to generate your output.

Classy

Classy is a python module that utilizes AI to classify natural language into categories for processing with other models and tools, such as Llama and Dall-E, Search_Scrape, and CustomChat.
